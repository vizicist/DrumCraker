Underlying samples
------------------
The WAV samples were recorded by Vincent Sermonne (http://tchackpoum.fr/).
The readme does not attach a formal license to the raw recordings; instead
it points to his site, which has a "donate" link at the bottom of the page:

    "If you wish to donate money for this, please visit the link above."

In short: the DrumGizmo packaging is WTFPL (do anything you want); the
sample recordings come courtesy of Vincent Sermonne with a donation
request rather than a stated license. If you use this kit in a project,
consider donating to Vincent Sermonne for the samples.
